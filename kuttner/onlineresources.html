Walther Holtzmann Card Files

SKIMCL is pleased to make available to scholars around the world the research notecards of the late Walther Holtzmann, which document the presence of papal decretals in various decretal collections of the second half of the 12th century. Other Resources on the Web: Though not exhaustive, the following list includes websites curated by experts in the field and by affiliates of SKIMCL that are of use for research in medieval canon law.
